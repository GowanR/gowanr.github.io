Rust-Opt

Rust-Opt is an n-dimentional genetic particle swarm optimizer written in Rust. The algorithm uses genetic selection to meta-optimize the genetic particle swarm algorithm. Can operate under the following benchmarkes in n-dimentions: Rastrigin, Sphere, Rosenbrock, McCormick, Ackley, and DropWave. Also implements a few other optimization algorithms to compare preformance.

pid-java

A highly configurable PID controller written in Java. Includes some features to prevent integral windup, steady state error checking within a tolerance, and the ability to recursivly ramp setpoints. This is made for the FIRST Robotics Competition for teams who want a little more control, but don't have the money for Talon-SRXs.
